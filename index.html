Lamborghini

Roof

The roof features larger air intakes compared to previous models and accentuated aerodynamic profiles. The hardtop is made with an innovative high-pressure RTM molding technology.

Winfield

The windshield is large and wraparound, providing a wide frontal view. The windshield design is aerodynamic and sleek, in line with the car's aesthetic. It is made of laminated glass, which is impact-resistant and helps protect vehicle occupants in the event of an accident.

Tailight

The taillights of a Lamborghini Aventador are designed to be highly visible and complement the car's sporty and aerodynamic appearance. They feature LED lights that offer greater energy efficiency and brighter illumination compared to conventional lights.

Headlight

The front headlights of a Lamborghini Aventador are designed to complement the car's sporty and aerodynamic aesthetics, with angular and aggressive shapes that make it easily recognizable.

Wheels

The Lamborghini Aventador is equipped with large-sized tires to ensure maximum grip in any condition. The front tires measure 255/35 R19, while the rear tires measure 335/30 R20. The wheels of the Aventador are highly durable and designed to withstand high speeds and extreme driving conditions.

Bumper

The Lamborghini Aventador's carbon fiber bumpers are designed to improve aerodynamics, reduce air resistance, and enhance speed. Strategically placed air vents also provide better engine cooling.
